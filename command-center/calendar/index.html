💰 Funding & deadline cycles

The part no weather site shows — when the federal money clock matters.
  • 🔁
    SAM.gov registration renewal — annual
    Lapses lock you out of federal contracts & grants. Renew ~60 days before expiry.
  • 🏛️
    FEMA Public Assistance & HMGP — post-declaration
    Application windows open after a disaster is declared and close fast. Watch the live map for new declarations.
  • 🏗️
    HUD CDBG-DR — appropriation-driven
    Funds follow major disasters via Congressional appropriation; NOFAs and action-plan deadlines follow.
  • 📅
    Federal fiscal year — Oct 1
    Grant cycles, BRIC/mitigation notices, and agency budgets reset. Position before Q4.
