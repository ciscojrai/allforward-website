Kentucky hazard season FAQ

When is flood season in Kentucky?

In Kentucky, flood season typically runs February–July. Federal recovery funding windows (FEMA Public Assistance, Hazard Mitigation, and HUD CDBG-DR where appropriated) open after a declaration and close quickly — preparation before the season matters.

In Kentucky, tornado season typically runs March–May. Federal recovery funding windows (FEMA Public Assistance, Hazard Mitigation, and HUD CDBG-DR where appropriated) open after a declaration and close quickly — preparation before the season matters.
